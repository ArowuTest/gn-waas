1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing or using the Ghana National Water Audit & Assurance System ("GN-WAAS" or "the System"), you agree to be bound by these Terms of Use. If you do not agree to these terms, you must not access or use the System.

These Terms of Use are issued by the Ministry of Water Resources & Sanitation of the Republic of Ghana and apply to all portals, APIs, mobile applications, and services forming part of GN-WAAS.

Important: Access to GN-WAAS is restricted to authorised personnel only. Unauthorised access is a criminal offence under the Electronic Transactions Act, 2008 (Act 772) and the Cybersecurity Act, 2020 (Act 1038).

2. Authorised Users

Access to GN-WAAS is restricted to the following categories of authorised personnel:

  • GN-WAAS System Administrators: Ministry-appointed technical administrators
  • Audit Managers: Personnel responsible for overseeing audit operations
  • GRA Auditors / Officers: Ghana Revenue Authority personnel assigned to water sector VAT compliance
  • Field Supervisors & Officers: Personnel conducting physical meter verification
  • GWL Management & Supervisors: Ghana Water Limited personnel managing billing cases
  • MOF Auditors: Ministry of Finance personnel with oversight access
  • PURC Representatives: Public Utilities Regulatory Commission oversight personnel
  • MDA Users: Ministerial Departments and Agencies with read-only access

User accounts are provisioned by the System Administrator and are non-transferable. Each account is tied to a specific individual and role.

3. Permitted Use

Authorised users may use GN-WAAS solely for the following purposes:

  • Performing water audit and assurance functions within their assigned role and district scope
  • Reviewing and responding to audit cases, anomaly flags, and compliance reports
  • Conducting GPS-locked field verification of water meters and accounts
  • Generating and downloading regulatory reports as authorised by their role
  • Managing field officer assignments and job dispatch within their supervisory scope

4. Prohibited Conduct

Users must not:

  • Access data outside their assigned district or role scope
  • Share login credentials with any other person
  • Attempt to bypass, disable, or circumvent any security control
  • Export, copy, or transmit system data to unauthorised parties or systems
  • Modify, falsify, or tamper with audit records, meter readings, or evidence
  • Use the system for any purpose other than official GN-WAAS audit functions
  • Introduce malware, scripts, or automated tools not authorised by the System Administrator
  • Disclose system architecture, credentials, or security configurations to third parties

Criminal Liability: Violations of these prohibitions may constitute offences under the Electronic Transactions Act, 2008 (Act 772), the Cybersecurity Act, 2020 (Act 1038), and the Criminal Offences Act (Act 29). Offenders will be reported to the appropriate authorities.

5. Account Security

Users are responsible for:

  • Maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials
  • Logging out of the system after each session
  • Reporting any suspected unauthorised access to the System Administrator immediately
  • Ensuring their registered device (for field officers) is not shared with other personnel

The Ministry reserves the right to suspend or terminate any account where a security breach is suspected, without prior notice.

6. Intellectual Property

GN-WAAS, including its software, algorithms, reports, and all content generated by the system, is the property of the Republic of Ghana, administered by the Ministry of Water Resources & Sanitation.

Users may not reproduce, distribute, or create derivative works from any GN-WAAS content without written authorisation from the Ministry.

GN-WAAS is built on open-source components. The source code is available at github.com/ArowuTest/gn-waas under the terms of the applicable open-source licences.

7. Disclaimer of Warranties

GN-WAAS is provided "as is" for official government audit purposes. While the Ministry takes all reasonable steps to ensure accuracy and availability, we do not warrant that:

  • The system will be uninterrupted or error-free at all times
  • All data sourced from GWL's billing replica is complete or accurate
  • Anomaly flags constitute conclusive evidence of fraud without further investigation

Audit findings generated by GN-WAAS are indicative and must be reviewed by qualified personnel before enforcement action is taken.

8. Limitation of Liability

To the maximum extent permitted by Ghanaian law, the Ministry of Water Resources & Sanitation shall not be liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from the use of or inability to use GN-WAAS, including but not limited to loss of data, business interruption, or reputational harm.

9. Termination

The Ministry may suspend or terminate a user's access to GN-WAAS at any time, with or without notice, for:

  • Violation of these Terms of Use
  • Cessation of employment or authorised role
  • Security concerns or suspected misuse
  • System decommissioning or restructuring
