🇬🇭   National Revenue Assurance Initiative · 2026

Restoring Financial Integrity to Ghana's Water Sector

GN-WAAS is the Government of Ghana's independent audit and assurance platform, mandated to quantify, investigate, and recover non-revenue water losses across all districts served by Ghana Water Limited.

Statutory Mandate: Established under the authority of the Ministry of Water Resources & Sanitation in coordination with the Public Utilities Regulatory Commission (PURC) and the Ghana Revenue Authority (GRA) to enforce Act 1151 E-VAT compliance across the water sector.

Our Mandate
Three Pillars of National Water Assurance

GN-WAAS operates as an independent oversight layer — it does not replace GWL's billing system. It audits it.

Pillar 01 — Financial Truth

Shadow Billing & Revenue Reconciliation

An independent shadow ledger recalculates every customer bill using official 2026 PURC tariffs. Any account where GWL's actual bill deviates by more than 15% from the shadow calculation is automatically flagged for investigation and GRA compliance review.

Pillar 02 — Physical Truth

Statistical Flow Analysis & Leak Detection

Night-flow analysis (2:00–4:00 AM) across District Metered Areas identifies anomalies that exceed 30% of daily average flow. Pattern recognition distinguishes physical infrastructure leaks from deliberate commercial theft operations.

Pillar 03 — Operational Truth

GPS-Locked Field Verification

Field officers operate under a tamper-proof mobile protocol requiring GPS proximity confirmation, OCR-based meter photo capture, and digital signatures. Every audit record is cryptographically hashed and submitted to GRA's VSDC API for legal certification.

How GN-WAAS Enforces Accountability

Every audit action is traceable, immutable, and legally certified before it is recorded.

  • 🔍
    Anomaly Detection Sentinel engine continuously monitors shadow bill variances, night-flow patterns, and ghost account indicators across all 1.2M accounts.
  • 📋
    Audit Case Management Flagged anomalies are converted into structured audit cases, assigned to field teams, and tracked from detection through to resolution and revenue recovery.
  • 📍
    Field Verification GPS-fenced mobile audits require officers to be physically present at the meter location. Device authentication prevents proxy submissions.
  • 🏛️
    GRA Compliance Lock No audit report is finalised until GRA's VSDC API returns a signed QR code. This creates prosecution-ready, tamper-proof legal evidence.
  • 📊
    Revenue Recovery Tracking Confirmed recoveries are tracked against the national NRW target. District-level heatmaps provide the Ministry with real-time fiscal intelligence.
